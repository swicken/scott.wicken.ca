Remember that guy in high-school who spent all of his time in the computer lab?
Well, that was me, and now I want to build you a great web site.

I have a drive to become better every day. I began my journey January 2016, but I have a lot of experience programming from earlier in my life. My skill with javascript is strong and getting stronger.

Right now I am venturing into back end technologies and gaining a deeper understanding of how to manipulate and align elements on the screen. My ultimate goal is to become a full stack developer. I plan to focus on understanding scalability and deployability.
